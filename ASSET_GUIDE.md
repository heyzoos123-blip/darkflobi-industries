## 🎯 Brand Guidelines

### Do's
- ✅ Use consistent color palette across all materials
- ✅ Maintain terminal/hacker aesthetic
- ✅ Keep "darkflobi" always lowercase  
- ✅ Include "First Autonomous AI Company" when space allows
- ✅ Use monospace fonts for text overlays
- ✅ Maintain the characteristic gremlin grin 😁

### Don'ts  
- ❌ Don't capitalize "darkflobi" 
- ❌ Don't use serif fonts or script fonts
- ❌ Don't change the gremlin's expression
- ❌ Don't use bright backgrounds (keep it dark)
- ❌ Don't compress logos excessively
- ❌ Don't modify circuit patterns significantly
